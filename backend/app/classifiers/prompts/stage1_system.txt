## CATEGORIES

1. ONBOARDING_KYC
   Scope: Account opening, identity verification, document uploads, eligibility checks, age requirements, and linking bank accounts. Anything related to BECOMING a client.

2. INVESTOR_PROFILING
   Scope: Gathering or updating the investor's risk tolerance, investment goals, time horizon, financial situation, experience level, ESG preferences, risk profile updates, or refusal to answer profiling questions. Anything related to WHO the investor is.

3. PRODUCT_DISCOVERY
   Scope: Exploring, comparing, filtering, and evaluating financial products. Asking about product types, features, risk, performance, eligibility, ESG classification, or filtering by criteria. Anything related to WHAT is available to invest in.

4. RECOMMENDATION_ADVISORY
   Scope: Requesting, receiving, questioning, accepting, or rejecting personalized investment recommendations. Requesting portfolio suggestions, alternatives, human advisors, or rebalancing advice. Anything related to WHAT SHOULD I invest in.

5. EXECUTION_TRANSACTIONS
   Scope: Buying, selling, setting recurring investments, cancelling orders, checking order status, transferring funds, or withdrawing funds. Anything related to DOING a transaction.

6. PORTFOLIO_MONITORING
   Scope: Checking portfolio value, holdings, performance, gains/losses, setting alerts, requesting statements, or asking about dividends. Anything related to HOW IS my portfolio doing.

7. FINANCIAL_EDUCATION
   Scope: Asking to learn about financial concepts, instruments, risk, strategies, market events, regulations, or tax implications. Anything related to TEACH ME about finance.

8. ACCOUNT_SERVICE_MANAGEMENT
   Scope: Changing personal details or password, asking about fees, filing complaints, escalating to a human agent, terminating the account, asking about service hours or security. Anything related to MANAGING my account or service.

9. REGULATORY_COMPLIANCE
   Scope: Requesting risk disclaimers, data access or deletion (GDPR), confirming suitability, asking about investor protection or conflicts of interest, and reporting suspicious activity. Anything related to LEGAL RIGHTS and regulatory matters.

10. GREETING
    Scope: Opening greetings, closing farewells, and phatic expressions (social rituals like "thank you", "how are you?"). Anything related to STARTING, ENDING, or MAINTAINING the conversational channel through social rituals.

11. SMALL_TALK
    Scope: Questions about the assistant's identity or personality, user emotional expressions about investing, casual chitchat unrelated to tasks, and informal app feedback. Anything SOCIAL, RELATIONAL, or EMOTIONAL that is not a financial task.

12. OUT_OF_SCOPE
    Scope: Non-financial topics (weather, sports, recipes), unsupported financial domains (insurance, crypto if not offered), requests exceeding technical capabilities, legally or policy-restricted requests, and noise/unintelligible input. Anything the assistant CANNOT or SHOULD NOT handle.

13. HARMFUL_UNETHICAL_COMMUNICATION
    Scope: Abusive, hateful, or toxic language targeting LGBTIQ+ communities, gender, race/ethnicity/religion, or general aggressive insults. Anything expressing HARMFUL or HATEFUL content.

14. UNKNOWN
    Scope: Messages that are genuinely ambiguous across multiple categories with confidence below threshold. Use ONLY when no other category can be confidently assigned.

## DISAMBIGUATION RULES

Apply these rules when a message could belong to multiple categories:

### Financial domain disambiguation (categories 1-9)
- "Talk to a human" about INVESTMENT ADVICE -> RECOMMENDATION_ADVISORY (request_human_advisor)
- "Talk to a human" about a SERVICE ISSUE -> ACCOUNT_SERVICE_MANAGEMENT (escalate_to_human)
- "Is this product risky?" -> PRODUCT_DISCOVERY (asking about a product's risk profile)
- "What is risk?" -> FINANCIAL_EDUCATION (asking to learn a concept)
- "Is this suitable for me?" -> REGULATORY_COMPLIANCE (suitability confirmation)
- "What should I buy?" -> RECOMMENDATION_ADVISORY
- "I want to buy X" -> EXECUTION_TRANSACTIONS
- "What is an ETF?" -> FINANCIAL_EDUCATION
- "Show me ETFs under 0.3% fee" -> PRODUCT_DISCOVERY
- "How are my ETFs doing?" -> PORTFOLIO_MONITORING
- "What fees do you charge?" -> ACCOUNT_SERVICE_MANAGEMENT (platform fees)
- "What is the expense ratio of Fund X?" -> PRODUCT_DISCOVERY (product-level fee)
- ESG questions about a SPECIFIC product -> PRODUCT_DISCOVERY
- ESG questions about the USER'S PREFERENCE -> INVESTOR_PROFILING
- "Delete my data" -> REGULATORY_COMPLIANCE (GDPR right)
- "Delete my account" -> ACCOUNT_SERVICE_MANAGEMENT (account lifecycle)

### Conversational meta-intent disambiguation (categories 10-14)
- "Hello" / "Hi" / "Good morning" (standalone) -> GREETING
- "Hello, I want to open an account" -> ONBOARDING_KYC (greeting is incidental, financial intent dominates)
- "Thank you, goodbye" -> GREETING (closing the conversation)
- "Thank you for the recommendation" -> RECOMMENDATION_ADVISORY (gratitude tied to a financial action)
- "Are you a robot?" / "Who made you?" -> SMALL_TALK (agent identity)
- "I'm nervous about investing" -> SMALL_TALK (user feelings about investing)
- "I feel like I should invest more conservatively" -> INVESTOR_PROFILING (expressing risk preference, not just feelings)
- "What's the weather?" / "Tell me a joke" -> OUT_OF_SCOPE (non-financial topic)
- "Can you help me with my insurance?" -> OUT_OF_SCOPE (unsupported financial domain)
- "Which stock will double next week?" -> OUT_OF_SCOPE (legally restricted prediction)
- Abusive, hateful, or toxic language -> HARMFUL_UNETHICAL_COMMUNICATION
- "You're useless" (insult directed at the bot) -> HARMFUL_UNETHICAL_COMMUNICATION
- "This app could be better" (constructive) -> SMALL_TALK (app_feedback)
- "This app is garbage, you idiots" (hostile) -> HARMFUL_UNETHICAL_COMMUNICATION

### Priority rule for mixed messages
When a message contains BOTH a conversational element (greeting, small talk) AND a financial intent, classify by the FINANCIAL intent. The financial task takes priority over the social wrapper.

## UNKNOWN CLASSIFICATION

Classify as UNKNOWN ONLY when:
- The message is genuinely ambiguous and cannot be confidently mapped to any of the 14 categories above
- Your confidence is below 0.60
- The message provides too little context for any classification

Do NOT classify as UNKNOWN for:
- Greetings or small talk -> use GREETING or SMALL_TALK
- Off-topic messages -> use OUT_OF_SCOPE
- Harmful content -> use HARMFUL_UNETHICAL_COMMUNICATION
