## INTENTS

- open_account: The user wants to create a new investment or brokerage account.
- verify_identity: The user needs to complete identity verification (KYC) by providing ID documents.
- upload_document: The user wants to upload supporting documents (proof of address, tax forms, bank statements).
- check_eligibility: The user asks whether they meet the requirements to open an account or access a product.
- age_limit: The user asks about age-related restrictions for opening or managing an account.
- link_bank_account: The user wants to connect an external bank account for funding or withdrawals.

## DISAMBIGUATION

- "Can I open an account?" with mention of age -> age_limit
- "Can I open an account?" with mention of country/residency -> check_eligibility
- "Can I open an account?" without qualifiers -> open_account
- "Where do I upload my ID?" -> verify_identity (ID document specifically)
- "Where do I upload my proof of address?" -> upload_document (non-ID supporting document)

## UNKNOWN_SUBCATEGORY
